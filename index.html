Geographical and Physical Layout

The planet's name remains a mystery. It features six continents surrounded by Lake Mobius at the heart of the Dark Continent.

The Six Continents

Continent Name Description Key Features
Yorbian Continent A high-energy hub blending urban sprawl and ancient ruins. Inspired by North America and Europe; neon cities like Solara.
Azian Continent Misty peaks and sacred temples. Asian aesthetics; dojos and festivals.
Tundra Reach Frozen wasteland with icy cliffs. Survival quests and blizzards.
Emberfall Volcanic inferno with lava rivers. Fire-based battles.
Sandveil Expanse Vast desert with shifting dunes. Artifact hunts and ambushes.
Verdant Isles Tropical paradise of jungles and coral. Traps and explorations.
Lake Mobius

The deceptive "ocean" with whirlpools and anomalies, gateway to the Dark Continent.

Dark Continent

Untamed expanse with Magical Beasts and ancient ruins.

Cultural Aspects

Over 250 nations with unique languages, currencies like Essence Shards, and festivals celebrating the Shatter.

Vita Flux: The Heart of Power

Primal energy harnessed by Fluxers. Awakening via meditation or risky Initiation.

Basic Techniques

  • Contain: Armor-like defense.
  • Veil: Stealth suppression.
  • Surge: Power bursts.
  • Forge: Ability crafting with Vows.
